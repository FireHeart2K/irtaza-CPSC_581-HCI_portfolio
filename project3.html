Project 3
Phone Down Game

Overall Description

Group: Dheeraj Kumar, Leslie Nguyen , Mahnoud Abdelaty, and Me :3

Technology has aided in advancing human connects in more meaningful ways by giving society different channels to connect. Social media has transformed videos and image sharing as a meaning of staying close to one another. However, it has also created more distance in many relationships. Things posed on social media may not represent the whole truth and people are growing addicted to their devices uninterested in real connections. No one wants to talk to someone who is barely paying attention to them on their phone.

That is why our group created a digital platform to keep you off your phone! Our platform is meant to be used in a group setting to improve communication, conversation, collaboration. All people in the group turn on platform and enter in your information. Then they place their phones face down and when the phone is picked up it will showcase a red screen indicating it has been handled. The platform prevents the user from getting distracted and picking up their phone out of habit so that they can pay attention to real life communication and be able to have meaningful conversations with the ones that truly matter. Thus, allowing important collaboration.

Concept Sketches

Write-up for the concept sketches

Exploring the concept of communication, collaboration, and conversation many of these ideas are different ways to facilitate communication. Brainstorming different channels of communication help facilitate the idealization process. Platforms for text communication include sketches 2,3,4. Where users are connected is various ways to others and have conversation by sending each other test messages. Image based forms of communicate include sketches 7,9,10 where users collaborate and interact to create a unique image that gets shared to each party. Sketches 1 and 8 are game play-based ways of communication where users collaborate to create conversation through their actions on the platform.

These initial ideas were taken into group discussion, and we decided to go for another group members idea. The final idea is a platform that a group can all access that enables real life communication by keeping people off their devices. It is collaborative because is it meant to be used in a group setting by many people in a friend outing and help assist conversation by ensuring that people are not distracted by their phone. This idea is over a creative take in a way to be connected to one another.

Write-up for detailed sketches

The sketches combine ideas from all group members. Each detailed sketch surrounds the idea of communication through utilizing different modes of interaction with users. Some utilize text-based exchange and other only use images or application contact to help users connect with each other.

The group ended up choosing the idea of a digital platform that keeps people off their devices to strength real life conversations (sketch 5). The platform is a novel way to keep people attentive to each other and motivated to engage in building meaningful relationships with each other.
